# Vestia

---

## Why Vestia ?

After discovering the world of homelabing and how people care about their privacy, I started thinking about control — not just over servers, but over work itself. Big companies should not have their hands on your projects, your tasks, your data. Vestia was born from that frustration. Self-hosted alternatives already exist, but most of them sacrifice usability for sovereignty. Vestia aims to offer both : a clean, modern interface and full control over where your data lives.

## What Vestia does (and doesn't do)

| What Vestia does                                   | What Vestia doesn't                    |
| -------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Provide a clean and modern UI                      | Store your data on third-party servers |
| Let you create unlimited boards for free           | Restrict features behind a paywall     |
| Give access to all features without a subscription | Collect or monetize your usage data    |
| Send notifications via Discord webhooks            |                                        |
| Keep your data on your own infrastructure          |                                        |
